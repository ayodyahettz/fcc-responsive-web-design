Eton Mess

Here's a simple recipe for a quick 4-ingredient dessert

Ingredients

  • Strawberries - 500g
  • Whipping Cream - 250ml
  • Sugar - 15g
  • Meringue Chunks - 200g

Instructions

  1. Cut Strawberries into chunks, or slice them based on preference.
  2. Whip cream, adding sugar to taste if preferred.
  3. Combine meringue, Strawberries and cream.
  4. Serve Chilled.
